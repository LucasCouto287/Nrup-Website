BLOCKCHAIN MUSIC USECASE STUDY

What is the Usecase?

BLOCKCHAIN MUSIC USECASE STUDY is a collaborative effort between me and 2 other students. We are proposing a new solution to manage the rights property of the music and art industry. Based on a blockchain platform to guarantee the right distribution of royalties transparency and transactions on payments of royalties.

Inspiration

All revenues from their product are distributed to every intermediary that work with them. Thus, artists in today's world of streaming and digital publishing are getting short-sided with the distribution of the money from their work. .

What it Does

Our solution will be in the form of a platform, with a public permissioned design, creating a safe environment for deal with multipart contract in the music industry. Also, it will be permissioned, in order to identify members in the network, attributing the precise ownership and property of the songs or content. In addition, identifying users can to avoid problems such as illicit content.

What is a AUCTION DAPP?

Auction for Fundraising for a Social Cause. Problem Statement: Consider the problem of Chinese auction or penny social. We will refer to it as simple ‚ÄúAuction.‚Äù It is a conventional approach used for fundraising for a cause. The organizers collect items to be auctioned off for raising funds.

Why Auction?

Before the auction, the items for auctions are received and arranged each with a bowl to place the bid. A chairperson is a special person among the organizers. She/he heads the effort and is the only person who can determine the winner by random drawing at the end of the auction. A set of bidders buy sheets of tickets with their money. The bidder‚Äôs sheet has a stub that identifies the bidder‚Äôs number, and tokens bought. The bidders examine the items to bid, place one or more tickets in the bowl in front of the items they desire to bid for until all the tickets are used. After the auction period ends the chairperson, collects the bowls, randomly selects a ticket from each item‚Äôs bowl to determine the winning bidder for that item. .

Why?

The item is transferred to the winning bidder. Total money collected is the fund raised by the penny social auction.
